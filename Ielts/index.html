IELTS Speaking Practice

Part 1 - Introduction Questions

Practice answering these common questions:

  • Can you tell me about your hometown?
  • What do you do for work or study?
  • What are your hobbies and interests?
  • How do you usually spend your weekends?

Part 2 - Cue Card Topic

Describe a memorable journey you have taken.

You should say:

  • Where you went
  • Who you traveled with
  • What made it memorable
  • And explain how you felt about this journey

You have 1 minute to prepare and 2 minutes to speak.

Speaking Tips:

  • Use a variety of vocabulary and avoid repetition
  • Speak clearly and at a natural pace
  • Use linking words to connect your ideas
  • Give detailed examples to support your points
  • Practice expressing opinions and giving reasons
